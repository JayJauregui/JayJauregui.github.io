How do we use AI in our everyday life?

The most popular uses of AI in an everyday settings are the ones that we usually take for granted and forget are actually AI.
These examples include:
  • Amazon Alexa
  • Apple's Siri
  • Apple Maps / Google Maps and their Navigation
  • Face ID on Mobile Devices

As pictured to the left, navigational applications like Apple maps and Google Maps uses the help artificial intelligence to look over things like traffic flow to help show the fastest route you can take and to predict your time of arrival.


Many others may use AI to fight their boredom or lonliness with the use of AI chatbots. Although, the use of these bots would be more of a concious decision than not knowing they're using an AI service.
