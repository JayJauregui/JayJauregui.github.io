What is AI?

The ever prevalent use and implementation of AI has sky-rocketed in the 21st century. Particularly, the mid 2020's. It seems like it's getting added into absolutely everything you can think of.
Some examples include:
  • Banking Apps
  • Website Customer Service
  • Self-Driving Cars
  • Digital Assistants
Artificial Intelligence is the science of making machines that can think, learn and solve problems like and for humans.
